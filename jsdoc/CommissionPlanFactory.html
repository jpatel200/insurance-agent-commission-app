Class: CommissionPlanFactory

CommissionPlanFactory()

new CommissionPlanFactory()

Factory class for creating different Commission Plans based on the given input - Plan Type (e.g. 'A' or 'B'). Currently it supports two commission plans A & B but due to its design pattern ( Factory Design Patterns) it can be easily extended to support more commission plans without impacting other modules/classes
